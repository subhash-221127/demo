# Complaint / Case Management System (GovTech)
## Product Requirements Document (PRD)



## 1. Project Title

**GovTech Complaint / Case Management System**

## 2. Problem Statement

Government departments receive many complaints from citizens related to public services such as roads, sanitation, electricity, water supply, and other civic issues. Traditional complaint systems are often manual and inefficient, leading to delays, poor tracking, and lack of transparency.

Citizens usually do not receive timely updates about their complaints, while government officers struggle to manage and prioritize large numbers of cases.

The **Complaint / Case Management System** aims to solve these problems by providing a centralized digital platform where citizens can submit complaints, upload evidence, and track complaint status. Officers and administrators can manage complaints efficiently and generate reports for monitoring performance.



## 3. Objectives

The main objectives of the system are:

- Provide a centralized platform for submitting complaints.
- Improve transparency and accountability in complaint resolution.
- Allow citizens to track complaint status in real time.
- Enable officers to efficiently manage complaints.
- Allow users to upload evidence to support complaints.
- Provide reports and analytics for monitoring complaint trends.



## 4. Key Features

### Citizen Complaint Submission
Citizens can register and log in to the system and submit complaints using a complaint form.

### Evidence Upload
Citizens can upload images or documents as evidence related to the complaint.

### Complaint Assignment
Complaints are assigned to the appropriate department or officer responsible for resolving the issue.

### Status Tracking
Citizens can track the progress of their complaints through different stages:

- Submitted
- Under Review
- In Progress
- Resolved
- Closed

### Officer Dashboard
Officers can view assigned complaints, update complaint status, and add remarks.

### Reports and Analytics
Administrators can generate reports such as:

- Total complaints
- Department-wise complaints
- Complaint resolution statistics



## 5. System Modules

### Authentication Module
Handles user registration and login for citizens, officers, and administrators.

### Complaint Submission Module
Allows citizens to submit complaints through a complaint form.

### Evidence Upload Module
Allows users to upload supporting files related to complaints.

### Complaint Management Module
Allows officers to manage assigned complaints and update their status.

### Complaint Tracking Module
Allows citizens to monitor the status of their complaints.

### Reports and Analytics Module
Provides complaint statistics and analytical reports.

### Administration Module
Allows administrators to manage users, assign complaints, and monitor system performance.

## 7. User Roles

### Citizen
- Register and log in to the system
- Submit complaints
- Upload evidence

- View officer updates

### Officer
- View assigned complaints
- Review complaint details
- Update complaint status
- Add remarks

### Admin
- Manage users
- Assign complaints to officers
- Monitor complaint progress
- Generate reports and analytics



## 8. System Workflow

1. Citizen registers on the platform.  
2. Citizen logs into the system.  
3. Citizen submits a complaint with description and evidence.  
4. The system or admin assigns the complaint to an officer.  
5. The officer reviews the complaint.  
6. The officer updates the complaint status.  
7. Citizens track the complaint status through the dashboard.  
8. The officer resolves the complaint.  
9. Admin generates reports and monitors system performance.

## 11.Expected Outcome

The GovTech Complaint / Case Management System will provide a reliable and transparent platform for managing citizen complaints. The expected outcomes of the system include:

- Citizens can easily submit complaints through an online platform.
- Users can upload supporting evidence such as images or documents.
- Citizens can track the real-time status of their complaints.
- Officers can efficiently manage and resolve assigned complaints.
- Administrators can monitor complaint activities and generate reports.
